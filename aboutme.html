About Me


Biography

I recently started my career as a Junior High Social Studies teacher. I completed my undergraduate degree at Brigham Young University in 2019. At BYU, I studied History teaching and Teaching English as a Second Language.

During my time at BYU, I was assigned to student teach at Orem Junior High. After I completed my student teaching experience, I was hired to teach at OJHS. I have been at Orem Junior for the past year and a half as an 8th-Grade U.S. History teacher and a 9th-Grade World History/Geography teacher. I love learning about the past and recognizing the paths that brought us to our present reality.

During my first year teaching, I decided that I wanted to further my education while I was young. I have been working on a Masters of Education at the University of Utah. This Masters Degree is in Instructional Design and Educational Technology. I have completed almost two semesters in the IDET program at the University of Utah. I'm excited to see what else I learn in this program.
